Account abstraction support in Aomi is coming soon.

When it lands, this page will document the public API for session keys, sponsored gas, and batched operations. Until then, transactions follow the standard [non-custodial](/concepts/non-custodial-wallets) flow: the App stages a transaction, the host simulates it, and you sign it in your own wallet.
